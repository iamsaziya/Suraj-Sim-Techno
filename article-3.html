Overview

A leading fabrication company from Aurangabad, founded as Suraj Steel Work in 1986 and later evolved into Suraj SIM Techno Works Pvt Ltd under the leadership of the next generation – Amol Surdia, Vishal Toshniwal, and Deepak Toshniwal. The company specializes in:

  1. Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEBs)
  2. Heavy structural fabrication
  3. Turnkey civil and mechanical engineering solutions
With a strong focus on innovation, durability, design precision, and timely delivery, they cater to major clients like RSB Industries, Nirmiti Industries, and more.
