Overview
Components:
  1. Purlins & Girts: Horizontal members supporting roof and wall panels.
  2. Eave Struts, Bracings & Sag Rods: Provide lateral support and transfer loads to primary frames.
Key Facts:
  1. Typically account for 15–20% of total steel used in a PEB structure.
  2. Cold-formed steel sections (C/Z shapes) dominate due to light weight and strength.
Trends & Opportunities:
  1. Mass production and cold roll-forming innovations driving cost efficiency.
  2. High demand in multi-span warehouses and large logistics parks.
  3. Bracing systems increasingly optimized using software-based load simulations.
Opportunity:
  1. High ROI as secondary systems reduce material cost while improving structural performance.
  2. Growing preference for galvanized sections due to durability and anti-corrosion needs.
