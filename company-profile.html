Suraj SIM – Beyond PEB

Suraj SIM is a premier PEB (Pre-Engineered Buildings) manufacturer in the Marathwada region. Our expertise goes beyond PEB — we deliver complete industrial infrastructure solutions under one roof as a Turnkey Project Partner.

We specialize in:

  • Industrial Civil Infrastructure
  • Manufacturing of Rolling Shutters, Gates & Automation
  • Heavy Industrial Fabrication Solutions

OUR VISION

Leading the Industry with Innovative, Efficient, and Customer-Centric Pre-Engineered Building Solutions

We aim to be a recognized leader in the Pre-Engineered Buildings industry by achieving excellence in performance. Our focus is on delivering innovative, precisely engineered, and efficient solutions that prioritize customer needs.
